See It In Action

Clean Interface Design

The Smart Habit Tracker features a clean, minimalist interface with a clear table showing your habits, their status, current streak, and available actions. The summary at the bottom shows your total habits and completion rate.

Adding New Habits

Click the "Add Habit" button to create a new habit. Enter the habit name and it will appear in your tracking table, ready for you to start building your streak.

View and Manage Your Habits

The "View Habits" button allows you to see all your current habits. Mark habits as complete, delete unwanted habits, or check your progress all from one convenient view.
