How To Use Smart Habit Tracker

1

Setting Up Your Account

Launch the application and create your personal profile. You can customize your settings, themes, and notification preferences from the settings menu.

  • Open the application after installation
  • Click "Create Profile" on the welcome screen
  • Enter your name and preferred settings
2

Creating Your First Habit

Add habits you want to track. Define the frequency, set goals, and choose whether you want reminders for each habit.

  • Click the "+" button on the main dashboard
  • Name your habit (e.g., "Morning Exercise")
  • Set frequency (daily, specific days, etc.)
  • Configure optional reminders
3

Daily Tracking

Open the app daily to mark your habits as completed. The calendar view provides a quick overview of your progress.

  • View your daily habits on the main screen
  • Tap the checkbox next to completed habits
  • Add notes to habits if needed (optional)
4

Analyzing Your Progress

Use the statistics tab to visualize your progress over time. Identify patterns and understand which habits you're succeeding with.

  • Navigate to the "Stats" tab
  • View completion rates for each habit
  • Check your weekly and monthly trends
  • Export data as CSV for deeper analysis
